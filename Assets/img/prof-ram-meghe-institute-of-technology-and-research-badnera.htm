About Prof Ram Meghe Institute of Technology and Research, Badnera

Latest News and Events

Prof. Ram Meghe Institute of Technology and Research (PRMITR) is an autonomous institute established in 1983, the institute is approved by AICTE. Additionally, the institute has received an A+ grade and a CGPA of 3.43 from the National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC) and some of its PRMITR UG programmes are accredited thrice by NBA. In 2023, the National Institutional Ranking Framework (NIRF) ranked Prof. Ram Meghe Institute of Technology and Research among the top 251-300 engineering colleges in India. 

Prof. Ram Meghe Institute of Technology and Research is affiliated to Sant Gadge Baba Amravati University, Amravati and offers about 20 UG and PG courses in various disciplines of engineering, management and computer applications. 

Prof. Ram Meghe Institute of Technology and Research offers various courses such as B.E, MBA, MCA, and ME. The PRMITR provides admission which is based on the performance of the candidates in the national or state level entrance exams such as MHT CET exam, CAT/ MAT/ XAT, GATE, MAH CET MCA. 

The PRMITR training and placement cell provides placement assistance and career guidance to its students. Prof. Ram Meghe Institute of Technology and Research facilities include a library, sports, auditorium, cafeteria, medical centre, transportation.

Prof. Ram Meghe Institute of Technology and Research Location

The institute is located in Badnera, Amravati, Maharashtra. Dr. Panjabrao Deshmukh Amravati Regional Airport is the nearest airport, located at a distance of 8.3 km that will take 11 minutes to reach the Prof. Ram Meghe Institute of Technology and Research. Badnera Junction is the nearest railway station located 3 km away from the institute. It will take 8 minutes from the railway station to reach the institute. The nearest bus stand is the Badnera bus stand, which takes 11 minutes to reach the campus by travelling 7 km.

    Prof Ram Meghe Institute of Technology and Research, Badnera Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ's)

    1: Are there any cultural events conducted at PRMITR?

    Yes, Every year the campus conducts cultural programmes called ESPERANZA / AASHAYEIN.

    2: Does the college provide any transportation facility to reach campus?

     Yes, the Prof. Ram Meghe Institute of Technology and Research offers transportation facilities for students and staff to reach campus.

    3: Does PRMITR offer a hostel facility to UG students?

    No, The institute does not provide any student hostel facility.

    4: Is Prof. Ram Meghe Institute of Technology and Research a private institute?

    Yes, Prof. Ram Meghe Institute of Technology and Research is a private institute as well as an autonomous institute. Prof. Ram Meghe Institute of Technology and Research is affiliated to Sant Gadge Baba Amravati University, Amravati. The institute has been accredited by NAAC with a grade of ‘A’ and is also approved by AICTE.
